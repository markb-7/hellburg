An Exlusive Whiskey Club

Well, if you have reached this page you were curious enough to know how Hellburg Whiskey Club came to be, so here is the greatest story you may ever hear. One day two guys, let's just call them a couple of geniuses for now, were sitting around drinking whiskey. After sampling a few different whiskeys, then a few more, these two guys came up with the oustanding idea of starting a whiskey club. It was so popular right off the bat that within minutes Hellburg Whiskey Club had their first memeber. From that point on there have been lines and hoards of people trying to get into this most prestigious club. People will get jealous when they find out you're in the club and they are not, but just hold your head up high and carry on. Memberships are issued on a very limited basis for this most exclusive club so sign up today!
